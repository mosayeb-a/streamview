A free and open-source offline Android app for navigating the Tehran Metro system efficiently.
Stream View is a Twitch client focused on providing the best viewing experience on mobile devices:

Features:

- Search & Discover – Easily find live streams, videos, and channels.
- Personalized Recommendations – Get video and stream suggestions based on your interests.
- Follow Your Favorites – Stay updated with the latest content from the channels you love.
- Smooth & Intuitive Browsing – Effortlessly explore trending and top live streams and categories.
- Open-source, privacy-respecting, and ad-free.
